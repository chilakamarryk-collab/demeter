
Zero-Waste Bakery Sustainability Project

Mission:
To reduce food waste in small bakeries using sustainability practices,
simple analytics, and technology tools.

Goals:
- Track leftover inventory
- Improve demand forecasting
- Encourage sustainable packaging
- Reduce environmental impact
- Explore AI-based waste reduction tools

Potential Future Features:
- Ingredient shelf-life tracking
- Carbon footprint estimation
- AI demand prediction
- Donation matching for excess food
- Sustainability dashboard for small bakeries
